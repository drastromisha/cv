About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Centro de AstrobiologÃ­a (CAB, CSIC), specializing in multiwavelength, spatially resolved spectral energy distribution (SED) fitting of high-redshift galaxies. My research focuses on exploiting JWST observations to study stellar populations, dust attenuation, star formation, and stellar mass assembly.

I obtained my PhD in Astrophysics with distinction in September 2023 from the National Centre for Nuclear Research (NCBJ, Poland), with a dissertation on dust attenuation in dusty star-forming galaxies. I have authored and co-authored 15 papers in high-impact international journals, advancing our understanding of dust attenuation laws, star formation calibrations, and the physical properties of dusty galaxies across cosmic time.
